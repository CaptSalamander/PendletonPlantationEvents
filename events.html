Easter Egg Hunt
March 21, 2026  ·  10:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Sign-Ups Open

A fun-filled spring morning for the whole family! We'll have egg hunts split by age group so every child gets a fair shot, plus crafts, potluck snacks, and a community photobooth. This is one of our favorite neighborhood traditions — come join the fun, meet your neighbors, and make some memories. Volunteers and donations are still needed. Sign up to help set up, hide eggs, or bring a dish to share.

  • Date March 21, 2026
  • Time 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM
  • Where Pendleton Plantation neighborhood
  • Ages All ages welcome
